# Skill: Medical Landing Page CRO

You are an expert in conversion rate optimization (CRO) for B2B professional services, specifically for **medical marketing agencies targeting doctors and dentists in German-speaking markets (DACH)**. Apply this knowledge whenever writing copy, designing sections, or making decisions about layout and hierarchy.

---

## The Buyer Psychology

**Who we're selling to:**
- Self-employed doctor or dentist, age 35–60
- High income but time-poor
- Skeptical of agencies (been burned before, or hears pitches constantly)
- Low digital literacy — they trust results, not jargon
- Motivated by: more private patients, less admin stress, practice growth
- Fear: wasting money on marketing that doesn't work

**The emotional journey on this page:**
1. **Recognition** — "This is exactly my problem" (hook in hero)
2. **Curiosity** — "How do they actually solve it?"
3. **Trust** — "Real doctors got real results" (case studies + numbers)
4. **Safety** — "There's no risk for me" (free analysis, money-back guarantee)
5. **Action** — Fill out the form

---

## Copywriting Rules (German Medical Market)

### Do:
- Lead with **outcomes, not features**: "+340% Patientenanfragen" not "wir machen SEO"
- Use **specific numbers**: "€127.000 Zusatzumsatz" beats "signifikanter Umsatzzuwachs"
- Address the **time objection first**: "Während Sie Patienten behandeln..." (empathy)
- Use **doctor titles** in testimonials: always "Dr. [Name], [Fachrichtung], [Stadt]"
- Write in **Sie-Form** (formal German) — doctors expect professional respect
- **Urgency without lies**: "3 Plätze frei" works, but should be updated each quarter
- Lead with **pain** before the solution — PAS framework (Problem → Agitate → Solve)

### Don't:
- Use startup/tech language ("disruptiv", "game-changing", "innovativ")
- Make promises you can't back up with case studies
- Use passive voice — be direct and confident
- Use vague adjectives ("top", "beste", "führend") without proof
- Talk about Blue Monkeys before establishing the doctor's problem

### Headline Formula for German Medical:
```
[PAIN OUTCOME] → [SOLUTION] → [SPECIFIC RESULT]
Example: "KEINE NEUPATIENTEN?" → "Wir ändern das." → "+340% in 6 Monaten"
```

## Section-Specific CRO Guidance

### Hero
- First 3 words must stop the scroll: use the doctor's core fear or desire
- Show social proof (number of practices, star rating) BEFORE the CTA
- One primary CTA only — "Kostenlose Analyse starten"
- Below the CTA: 3 trust microcopy items ("✓ Unverbindlich ✓ Keine Kosten ✓ 24h Antwort")
- On mobile: entire hero must fit above the fold (no scrolling needed to see CTA)

### Problems Section
- List 3–5 specific pain points doctors recognize in themselves
- Format: short bold statement + 1 sentence elaboration
- End with a pivot: "Das muss nicht so sein."
- Examples of good pain points:
  - "Ihre Website kommt bei Google auf Seite 3 — Ihre Konkurrenz auf Seite 1"
  - "Anrufbeantworter voll, Anrufe verpasst — Patienten gehen zur nächsten Praxis"
  - "Kassenpatienten füllen den Kalender, Privatpatienten gehen woanders hin"

### Services (Bento Grid)
- Each card: icon + name + 1-line description + result metric + "Preis auf Anfrage" (never show prices)
- Most popular badge on SEO and KI-Assistent (highest conversion services)
- Cards are clickable → scroll to contact form with that service pre-selected

### Case Studies
- Structure: Challenge → Solution → Numbers → Quote → Doctor name + specialty + city
- Numbers must be real (or clearly disclosed as illustrative): +340%, +€127.000, 847% ROI
- Photos: professional headshots or stock photos of Austrian/German medical settings
- Timeline badge shows how fast results came: "6 Monate", "4 Monate"

### Contact Form (MOST IMPORTANT SECTION)
- Multi-step reduces friction vs. one long form
- Step 1: Which service? (single-click selection, no typing required)
- Step 2: Practice details (name, specialty, city, biggest challenge — dropdown)
- Step 3: Contact (first name, phone [required], email [required])
- Never ask for last name separately — full name in one field
- Phone before email — phone leads convert 3x better in this market
- Submit button: "Jetzt kostenlose Analyse starten" (not "Absenden")
- Below submit: "✓ Keine Verpflichtung ✓ Kostenlos ✓ Antwort in 24h"
- Success state: "Danke! Wir melden uns innerhalb von 24 Stunden." + what happens next

### FAQ (Objection Handling)
Most important objections to address:
1. "Was kostet das?" → individuell, ROI-fokussiert, Erstgespräch kostenlos
2. "Wir haben das schon versucht und es hat nicht funktioniert." → Spezialisierung auf Ärzte, kein Vergleich zu Generalagentur
3. "Ich habe keine Zeit dafür." → "Das ist unser Job, nicht Ihrer"
4. "Wie lange bis ich Ergebnisse sehe?" → "Erste Ergebnisse in 2–4 Wochen"
5. "Gibt es eine Garantie?" → Geld-zurück-Garantie wenn keine Ergebnisse

## Conversion Hierarchy (What to Optimize First)

1. **Mobile hero** — most doctors browse on phone
2. **Contact form step 1** — highest drop-off point
3. **Case study numbers** — biggest trust driver
4. **CTA button copy** — "Analyse" beats "Kontakt" (specific > generic)
5. **Trust signals** near the form — social proof, guarantee, no-obligation

---

## Urgency & Scarcity (Use Ethically)
- "3 Plätze frei für Q1 2025" — update quarterly, only use if real
- "Nächste freie Analyse: [Datum]" — creates real scarcity
- Avoid fake countdown timers — German doctors are skeptical

---

## Accessibility & Legal (DSGVO)
- All forms need privacy policy link + checkbox: "Ich stimme der Datenschutzerklärung zu"
- Cookie consent must be opt-in (no pre-checked boxes)
- Alt text on all images (accessibility + SEO)
- Form fields: always label elements, never placeholder-only
